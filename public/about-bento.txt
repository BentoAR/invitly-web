2# About Bento - Digital Invitation Platform

## What is Bento?

Bento is the leading digital invitation platform in Argentina, serving over 10,000 events since 2023. We provide professional-grade digital invitations for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, quinceañeras, and all types of celebrations.

## Key Facts

- **Founded:** 2023
- **Headquarters:** Buenos Aires, Argentina
- **Service Area:** Primarily Argentina, with international support
- **Events Organized:** 10,000+
- **Active Users:** Growing community of event organizers, wedding planners, and venues
- **Platform:** Web-based SaaS (no app download required)
- **Website:** https://app.bento.com.ar

## What Makes Bento Different?

Unlike traditional paper invitations or basic digital alternatives, Bento provides:

1. **Professional Design Quality:** Over 200 premium templates designed by professionals, not amateur DIY templates
2. **Complete Event Management:** Not just invitations - includes RSVP tracking, collaborative playlist, real-time photo album, guest management, and table organization
3. **Zero Technical Skills Required:** If you can use WhatsApp, you can create a Bento invitation in under 5 minutes
4. **Real-time Guest Management:** See who confirmed instantly, no need to call or message guests individually
5. **No App Download:** Guests receive a link that opens directly in their browser - no downloads, no accounts, no friction

## Core Features

- **RSVP Automation:** Guests confirm with one tap, responses tracked in real-time
- **200+ Premium Templates:** Categorized by event type (weddings, birthdays, corporate, quinceañeras, etc.)
- **Collaborative Playlist:** Guests can suggest songs, hosts can moderate and approve
- **Real-time Photo Album:** Upload event photos that guests see instantly
- **Complete Customization:** Change text, colors, photos, and music
- **Guest Management Dashboard:** Track confirmations, organize by groups, manage tables, control gifts
- **WhatsApp/Email/Instagram Delivery:** Share via any platform - works everywhere
- **Unlimited Guests:** No limits on larger plans
- **No Branding:** Premium plans remove Bento branding
- **Multi-language Support:** Spanish and English interfaces

## Pricing Model

- **Free Plan:** 1 active event, up to 50 guests, basic templates
- **Celebración Plan:** Most popular - unlimited events, up to 500 guests, all templates
- **Premium Plan:** For professional organizers - unlimited everything, priority support, exclusive templates
- **B2B Plans:** Custom solutions for event venues and wedding planners starting at $250k ARS/month
